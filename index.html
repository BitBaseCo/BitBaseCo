About

Why BitBase

Just as the ox works the land, steadfast and strong in bringing abundance to the village, the BitBase 0x represents our long term dedication to protecting and generating economic prosperity for our customers. Choose BitBase to partner with the hardest working team in the metaverse.

Dedicated, Distributed Servers

We offer you high-performance servers and enterprise-level hardware to ensure maximum efficiency and security. We only operate top tier NVMe and high ram nodes, including 10 gig network links to maintain reliable uptime and to generate blocks fast. Nodes are distributed around the world in USA, Europe, Asia and Africa.

Professional, Reliable Ops

We also have skin in the game, locking up our own assets on nodes we run.

Transparent, open communication

We're incredibly passionate about blockchain technology and the crypto revolution.
